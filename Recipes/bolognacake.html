Bologna Cake

i'm so sorry

Totally fun appetizer for adults or kids. Watch their faces when you put this on the table.

Ingredients

  • 2 packages Cream Cheese (8 Ounce Packages)
  • 1 package Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing (1 Ounce Packet)
  • 12 ounces, weight Oscar Mayer Bologna
  • 1 can 8 Oz. Sharp Cheddar Aerosol Cheese
  • 1 whole Sprig Of Cilantro Or Pimento Stuffed Olive, For Garnish
  • 1 package Buttery Crackers, Like Townhouse Or Ritz

Directions

Step 1

  • Mix ranch dressing into softened cream cheese*, set aside.

    • Step 2

  • Place a slice of bologna on a plate, spread one Tablespoon or so of cream cheese on top of the slice, just like a cake, then add the second slice of bologna. Continue until all the slices are used.

    • Step 3

  • Then use the remainder of the cream cheese to finish ‘frosting’ the cake.

    • Step 4

  • When finished frosting the ‘cake’ squirt some of the aerosol cheese around the base and top of the cake to decorate it.

    • Step 5

  • Finish off with a sprig of cilantro on top or some pimento stuffed olives or … Let your imagination go.

    • Step 6

  • Cut into the ‘cake’ and place a small wedge on a buttery cracker to serve. And watch the adults swarm around the ‘cake’ and enjoy. This would be fun for a kid’s party as well.
